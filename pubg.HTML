PUBG MOBILE

PUBG Mobile is a simulated game that has a lot of open maps that allow you to choose the suitable terrain for free play.

What is the style of playing in PUBG Mobile?

It is played in a shooting style from the first or third person perspective, and the number of players reaches 100 players, each of whom aims to be the last survivor. Players can choose between servers where the player is alone and not in a specific team, or servers that allow two or four people to participate in a team. In all cases, the last person or team standing wins the match At the beginning of each match players jump from a parachute onto an island without any items in their pocket. Once they land, players can search buildings and other locations to find weapons, vehicles, and other equipment, which are distributed randomly across the map at the start of the match.

  • The third stage, which is the arena, and it is a match that contains only 8 players. You have to attack, and the maximum limit that is winning is 40 kills, and it contains maps such as: the warehouse. hangar domination and many more.
